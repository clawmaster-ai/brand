Professional brand identity compact lockup. Mark + wordmark only, no tagline. Dark background.

The mark from the reference image: hexagon with six glowing teal vertex nodes, radial spokes, dominant large teal center node. Deep navy-black background.

LAYOUT — horizontal, ultra-compact:
- Logo mark on LEFT, small — approximately 48px equivalent in a 400px-wide composition
- Text "OpenMaster" on RIGHT, same baseline as mark center
- Typeface: geometric sans-serif, light weight
- Color: teal (#00BCD4)
- Letter-spacing: 0.04em
- Size: approximately 1.8× mark height — mark and text feel like one unified unit
- No separator, no tagline, no decoration
- The mark and wordmark sit close together — tight but not cramped

OVERALL:
- Background: very deep navy (#02080E)
- The entire lockup is compact — could fit in a nav bar or app header
- Wide 16:9 frame, lockup centered and small, surrounded by vast dark space — this negative space is intentional and communicates scale
- The small lockup in infinite dark suggests: the platform is larger than any one product
